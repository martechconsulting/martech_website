What it is

Marketing automation that actually works for small business

Marketing automation is software that sends the right message to the right person at the right time — without you having to trigger it manually. A lead fills out your form, books a consult, or clicks a link, and a sequence of emails, texts, and tasks fires automatically based on what they did.

Done well, automation makes your business feel bigger than it is. You're responsive at 2am. You follow up five times without lifting a finger. You nurture a prospect for six months and they come back when they're ready.

We're a Keap Certified Partner — which means we've been trained and tested on the platform, not just tinkering with it. Keith has set up dozens of automation sequences for businesses across industries.

Worth knowing
Studies consistently show that businesses that follow up within 5 minutes of a lead inquiry are 21x more likely to close that lead than those who wait 30 minutes. Automation makes instant follow-up default — not exceptional.
