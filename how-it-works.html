How It Works

From first call to
live system.

Every project follows the same five-step process — designed to move fast, keep you informed, and make sure what we build actually works before it goes live.

1

Free 30-minute discovery call

We look at where your leads are coming from, where they go cold, and what tools you have. No pitch. No deck. Just an honest conversation about what would actually move the needle for your business — and whether we're the right people to do it.

Worth knowing
If we're not the right fit, we'll say so on this call. We'd rather refer you to someone better suited than take a project we can't nail.
2

Written proposal: flat price, firm scope

After the discovery call, you receive a written proposal with a specific scope of work, a flat price, and a firm timeline. No billable hours. No vague estimates that balloon. You know exactly what you're getting and exactly what it costs before you approve a single dollar of work.

Worth knowing
Price ranges on our packages page are real ranges. Where your project lands depends on complexity — the proposal locks in your specific number.
3

We build — you watch it take shape

Once approved, we get to work. For most projects, you'll see progress in the first 24–48 hours. We share builds as they develop, ask clarifying questions when we need your input, and keep you informed without requiring your constant attention. You don't have to manage us.

Worth knowing
The Starter Automation Setup is often live within 48 hours of approval. Larger projects take longer, but we communicate progress throughout — not just at the end.
4

Live walkthrough before launch

Before anything goes live, we walk you through the entire system — how it works, how to manage it, and what to watch. You test it yourself. You ask questions. You approve it. Only then do we flip the switch. We don't hand off a system you don't understand.

Worth knowing
For team deployments, we can run a training session as part of the walkthrough — so multiple people know how to operate the system, not just one.
5

Launch + post-launch support window

Every project launches with a support window built in — 30 days for the Starter package, 60 days for the Growth Stack. During that window, we fix anything that comes up, adjust based on how the system performs, and answer questions as your team gets comfortable using it.

After the support window
Facebook Ads management continues month-to-month. For other services, we offer ongoing retainer support for changes and additions — or you can take full ownership and manage it yourself. Your call.
