The origin story

Marketing automation saved a company during COVID. Then it became the company.

NH Rocks LLC was a digital platform connecting local New Hampshire businesses with residents. When COVID hit in 2020, the events and tourism model dried up overnight. The business pivoted hard, building out marketing automation and technology services for local businesses that suddenly needed help reaching customers online. The company grew 300% through the pandemic and achieved profitability within two years. That pivot became Martech Consulting.
