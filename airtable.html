What Airtable can do

Not just a database. A complete operations layer.

Most people discover Airtable and use it like a better spreadsheet. That's fine — but it misses the real power. A properly built Airtable base can run your entire operations workflow: track clients, manage projects, trigger automations, send emails, update records in other systems, and present data in custom interfaces for different team members.

Jonathan built a full destination marketing platform — managing local business listings, tourism content, campaign performance, and reporting — all inside Airtable, with public-facing interfaces powered by Softr and automation pipelines run through Make and Zapier. This was featured on the BuiltOnAir podcast (Season 20, Episode 5) as an example of what's possible at the advanced end of the platform.

Whether you need a simple CRM replacement or a full business operating system, we build Airtable systems that your team will actually use.
