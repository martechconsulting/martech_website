Why most ads fail

The reason your social media budget disappears

Most small businesses run Facebook ads by boosting posts, picking a broad audience, and hoping for the best. The platform is happy to take the money. But without a targeting strategy, tested creative, and a real funnel behind the click — you're paying for visibility that doesn't convert.

The other mistake: running ads without a follow-up system. Someone clicks your ad, fills out a form, and doesn't hear from you for two days. They've already moved on. Social media advertising and marketing automation have to work together.

Keith's background is in the wedding industry — a highly competitive, high-consideration purchase with a long sales cycle. If you can run profitable ads in that space, you can run them anywhere.
