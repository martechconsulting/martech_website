Questions

The ones we always get asked.

What does the $175/month actually cover?
Hosting, SSL, security, and up to 2 content changes per month. New phone number, updated service area, swap a service; that's all included. Bigger work like new pages ($200 each) or a booking integration ($350) is quoted separately, upfront.
Will this actually help me show up on Google?
Yes, but it takes a few months to build traction. We set up the right keywords, page structure, and service area from day one. Most clients see meaningful improvement in 3 to 6 months. The two-year term exists precisely because SEO needs time to work.
Do I need to own a domain already?
No. If you have one, great. We'll connect to it. If not, we'll help you register one. Domain registration is around $15 to $20 per year, paid directly to the registrar, not us.
