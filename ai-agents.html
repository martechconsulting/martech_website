What this actually is

An AI that knows your business — and talks to your tools.

AI agents aren't chatbots that answer generic questions. They're purpose-built assistants trained on your documentation, connected to your live data, and embedded directly into your Bubble app, Softr portal, or website.

We handle the full build: knowledge base setup, model selection, prompt engineering, and integration. You get an AI that sounds like you, knows your products, and works inside systems you already have.

AI Agents are an add-on to our core services — not a standalone offering. They work best when connected to an existing Airtable, Bubble, or automation stack we've built (or can build) for you.

Our process

How we build
your AI agent.

1
Discovery
We map the use case, what data the agent needs, where it lives, and who it talks to. We define success before touching any tools.
2
Knowledge Base
We structure your documentation, FAQs, and data into a knowledge base the model can query accurately and reliably.
3
Prompt Engineering
We write and test the system prompt that defines your agent's personality, guardrails, and response style — so it sounds like you.
4
Integration
We deploy the agent into your Bubble app, Softr portal, or website — wired to live Airtable or CRM data where needed.
