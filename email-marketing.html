The invisible problem

Why your email marketing isn't working

Email marketing has the highest ROI of any digital marketing channel — $36 returned for every $1 spent, on average. But that only works if your emails actually reach inboxes.

Most small businesses have deliverability problems they don't know about. Their domain reputation is damaged from sending to stale lists. Their DNS records are misconfigured — missing SPF, DKIM, or DMARC authentication. They're sending from a shared IP that other senders have abused.

The result: emails quietly routed to spam, or silently dropped. Open rates of 8% when they should be 35%. A complete program that looks functional but isn't reaching anyone.

Keith has spent years diagnosing and fixing exactly this problem — as an email deliverability specialist who built systems for industries with high-volume send requirements.

✕
